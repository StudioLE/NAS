about

The architecture society is a course based society within Northumbria students union. As a relatively new society we are always open to new suggestions and ideas from our members, your input will help the society to continually grow and improve.

The society was founded in 2007 as ArchSoc and the first website created in 2011.

In 2011, the society also established an Article25 student chapter for Northumbria University which focuses on disaster relief. Please see our Article 25 Page for more information.

In 2014 the society was rebranded as NAS (Northumbria Architecture Society)
